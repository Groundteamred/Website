RAMP SERVICE

  • To perform all ramp activities in operational areas for arrival, transit activities, and departure of all aircraft within the specified turn-around time during assigned tour of duty period.
  • Perform loading and unloading of baggage and cargo activities at baggage sorting areas, aircraft handling bay and arrival hall as per company and customer airline requirements.
  • Operate ground service equipment for ground handling activities.
  • Perform aircraft servicing functions during aircraft transit and night stop.
  • Perform aircraft towing, pushback and headset functions for aircraft movement. Ensure the bay is cleared of FOD before and after aircraft movement.
  • Ensure all ground service equipment are serviceable before usage. Adhere to all SOP & safety operation procedure.
  • Perform any other activities related to Ground Handling as required by the Company.
